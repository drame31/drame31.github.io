Flow

Three steps. Zero friction.

01

Set up your business

Register as owner, create your business with a unique slug, add your services, and generate the QR code in seconds.

02

Customers join via QR

They scan with any camera, enter their name, and instantly see their position and estimated wait time — updating live.

03

Staff serves in order

The panel shows the live queue. Call, start service, and complete — three clicks, no paper, no confusion.

Features

Everything your business needs

Real-time queue updates

Every status change propagates instantly to all connected clients via WebSocket — no polling, no delays.

QR walk-in check-in

Customers open any camera, scan the business QR, and they're in the queue. No app download, no account.

Actionable analytics

Peak hours, no-show rate, average service time, and weekly trends — with area charts and bar charts via Recharts.

Three independent roles

Owner configures the business, Staff manages the live queue, Customer only needs a phone and the QR.

Automatic wait estimation

Calculates each customer's wait time by summing the duration of all services ahead of them in the queue.

Secure JWT auth

Short-lived access token + refresh token rotation. Passwords hashed with bcrypt. Routes protected by role.

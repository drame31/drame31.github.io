03
// FEATURES

How the system works.

/01 · REAL-TIME

WebSocket updates

Every status change — a customer joining, being called, or served — is pushed to all connected clients immediately. No polling interval. No stale data.

/02 · CHECK-IN

QR walk-in

The business generates a QR code. Customers scan it with any phone camera and fill in a simple form. No account, no app download — works on any browser.

/03 · ANALYTICS

Owner dashboard

Peak hour heatmap, no-show rate, average wait time, and a 7-day trend — all calculated from real queue data and rendered with Recharts.

/04 · AUTH

Role-based access

Owner sets up services and generates QR codes. Staff manages the live queue. Customers join via the public link. Three roles, three separate views, one JWT-secured API.

/05 · ESTIMATION

Wait time calculation

Each position has a calculated expected wait based on how many services are ahead and their configured durations — updated automatically as the queue moves.

/06 · SECURITY

Secure auth flow

Short-lived access tokens with refresh token rotation. Passwords hashed via bcrypt. All protected endpoints verify role from the JWT payload.
